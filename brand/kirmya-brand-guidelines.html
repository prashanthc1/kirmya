Kirmya

The career-recovery platform. Warm referrals, real mentors, and an AI coach that knows your story — so the time between jobs is shorter, calmer, and more human.

01 — The name

Why “Kirmya”

Kirmya is a coined, ownable name (Kirmya.com) built to feel warm and human rather than corporate. It carries an echo of kindle — to spark something back to life — and reads as short, global, and easy to say. Where job boards sound like machinery, Kirmya sounds like a person on your side. The brand idea behind it: a setback is not the end of a career — it’s the moment before the rise.

02 — Logo

The mark: “The Rise”

The icon combines a bridge arc (connection, the path back) with a rising line that lifts into a spark (momentum, the rekindled career). It works as a full lockup or as a standalone app/favicon tile.

04 — Typography

Fraunces + Inter

Fraunces (a warm, optical serif) carries headlines and the wordmark — it feels human and dignified. Inter handles UI and body for clarity at small sizes.

06 — Voice & usage

How Kirmya speaks

Principles
  • Do lead with the human moment, then the practical step. Name the feeling, offer the plan.
  • Do be specific with real numbers once you have them — “14-day average to first interview” beats “faster.”
  • Don’t use fear, false urgency, or fabricated testimonials. Dignity over pressure.
  • Don’t sound like a job board or a hype machine. Calm, capable, on the user’s side.
